About Me

Dr. Ragini – Physiotherapist & Internationally Certified Yoga Instructor

Dr. Ragini is a highly accomplished Physiotherapist and Internationally Certified Yoga Instructor with over 12+ years of professional experience in healthcare, wellness, and rehabilitation. Accredited by the Ministry of AYUSH, India, and internationally certified by Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana (S-VYASA University), Bengaluru, she exemplifies authenticity and excellence in her practice.

Combining the scientific principles of physiotherapy with the timeless wisdom of yoga, Dr. Ragini offers a unique, evidence-based approach to health and well-being. She has successfully guided individuals of all age groups in improving flexibility, strength, posture, and stress management. Her programs are thoughtfully designed to be transformative, safe, and adaptable — especially for those managing chronic pain, musculoskeletal conditions, or undergoing rehabilitation after injury or surgery.

Over the years, Dr. Ragini has empowered countless students and clients to experience holistic healing through a blend of physical postures, breathing techniques, and mindfulness practices (Asana, Pranayama, and Dhyana). Her mission is to raise awareness and enhance the quality of life by seamlessly integrating physiotherapy with yoga.

Today, Dr. Ragini continues to inspire individuals to embrace yoga as a lifelong path to well-being, resilience, and inner harmony.
